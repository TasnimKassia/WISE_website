Our Story

Behind every successful woman is a tribe of other successful woman who have her back.

--Unknown

Our work centres around the advancement of women, girls, and gender-diverse students in STEM fields. We are an inclusive, student-led team that builds supportive networks and challenges industry biases through community-based events and programs. These initiatives include everything from hosting local workshops and panels to organizing national hackathons!
